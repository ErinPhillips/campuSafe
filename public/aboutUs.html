About Us

Our team at the College of Charleston want to thank you for using CampuSafe!

We are a small group of developers currently attending the College of Charleston. This project was developed using cutting-edge technology like BlockChain. We put in copius amounts of effort to get CampuSafe up and running for everyone to use, so we hope you enjoy it! If you have any suggestions, comments, or complaints, please don't hesitate to let us know through the contact information at the bottom of the page.
